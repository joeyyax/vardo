# Pricing

Pricing should feel predictable, fair, and calm.

Scope is priced to support real work — not punish growth or complexity.

---

## Starter — Free

For getting set up and doing real work.

- 1 user
- Up to **3 external clients**
- **Unlimited internal work**
- Unlimited projects
- Full feature access
- No time limit

Internal work never counts against limits.

---

## Solo — $12 / month

For independent professionals doing active client work.

- 1 user
- Unlimited external clients
- Unlimited internal work
- Time tracking
- Projects and tasks
- Proposals and contracts
- Invoicing
- Client portal
- Reporting and exports

---

## Team — $24 / month

For small teams working together.

- Multiple users
- Unlimited external clients
- Unlimited internal work
- Task assignments
- Shared visibility
- Client collaboration
- Everything in Solo

No per-seat pricing.

---

## What “Internal work” means

Every organization includes an **Internal** client.

Internal work is:
- Non-invoiced
- Fully tracked
- Included in reports
- Always free

Internal projects represent things like:
- Personal or internal tools
- Marketing and content
- Operations and admin
- Research and experimentation

Internal work is measured, not billed.

---

## What you don’t pay for

- Number of projects
- Number of tasks
- Time tracked
- Documents or invoices
- Exports or API access

Scope is priced to disappear once it’s working.

---

## Summary

Scope costs less than the mental overhead it replaces.

If you’re thinking about the work instead of the bill, it’s doing its job.
