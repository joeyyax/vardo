# Collaboration and Plans

Scope distinguishes between collaboration and shared ownership.

This distinction is intentional.

---

## Free Plan

- Single-user only
- No collaborators
- No shared visibility

Free is a single-player experience.

---

## Solo Plan

Solo supports collaboration without shared ownership.

Solo users may:
- Invite collaborators
- Work together on projects
- Track time independently
- Maintain separate billing and ownership

Each collaborator has their own account and plan.

Solo does not include:
- Shared billing
- Shared client ownership
- Shared external presentation

---

## Team Plan

Team represents a shared business entity.

Team includes:
- Shared ownership
- Shared billing
- Shared clients
- External presentation as a unit

Team is about structure, not headcount.

---

## Upgrade Signals

Users are encouraged to upgrade when they attempt:
- Shared billing
- Client portal ownership
- External-facing collaboration
- Organization-level control

Upgrades are intent-based, not numeric.

---

## Principle

> Scope charges for shared business ownership, not shared work.
