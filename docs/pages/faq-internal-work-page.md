# Internal Work

### What is “Internal” work?

Internal work represents non-client work done by your business.

This includes things like:
- Personal or internal projects
- Internal tools
- Marketing and content
- Operations and admin
- Research and experimentation

---

### Is Internal work free?

Yes.

Every organization includes unlimited Internal work.
It does not count toward client limits.

---

### Can I bill Internal work?

No.

Internal work is non-invoiced by design.

It is measured and reported, but never sent to a client.

---

### Does Internal work appear in reports?

Yes.

Internal work:
- Appears in totals
- Affects trends
- Contributes to utilization and cost

It is excluded only from client-facing views and invoices.

---

### Why track Internal work at all?

Because it’s real work.

Hiding internal effort leads to:
- Skewed profitability
- Poor planning
- Burnout masked as efficiency

Scope tracks internal work honestly.
