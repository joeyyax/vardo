# Support Philosophy

Support effort should scale with dependency, not headcount.

---

## Free Users

- Treated with respect
- Provided basic guidance
- Directed toward self-service
- Encouraged to upgrade when appropriate

Free does not include guarantees or priority support.

---

## Paid Users

- Higher reliability expectations
- Clear recovery paths
- Access to backups and exports
- Human support when needed

---

## Boundaries

Scope does not:
- Offer free infrastructure indefinitely
- Support misaligned use cases
- Hide limits or enforce them aggressively

---

## Principle

> Calm boundaries create sustainable support.
