# Account Dormancy Policy

Scope distinguishes between inactive, dormant, and abandoned accounts.

Data safety and trust take priority over cleanup.

---

## Definitions

- Active: user is logging in or modifying data
- Dormant: long period of inactivity
- Abandoned: no activity after explicit notice

---

## Dormant Accounts

For dormant free accounts:
- Background jobs may be paused
- Non-essential processing may stop
- Data remains intact
- Account fully reactivates on login

Dormancy is invisible to the user.

---

## Data Deletion

Scope does not delete user data without clear notice.

Deletion only occurs when:
- The user explicitly requests it, or
- Multiple notices are sent and ignored over a long period

Paid accounts are never auto-deleted.

---

## Principle

> We pause dormant accounts.  
> We do not surprise users.
