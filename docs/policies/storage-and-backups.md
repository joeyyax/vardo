# Storage and Backups

Storage and backups represent real infrastructure costs.

Scope handles them predictably and transparently.

---

## Storage Model

Storage is capped by plan.

Storage includes:
- Files and attachments
- Receipts
- Project uploads
- Backup data (compressed)

Storage does not include:
- Core database records (time entries, tasks, metadata)

---

## Plan-Based Storage

- Free: small cap (evaluation only)
- Solo: comfortable cap + automatic backups
- Team: generous cap + extended retention

Exact limits may change, but caps are always visible.

---

## Storage Add-ons

Paid plans may purchase additional storage.

- Flat-size add-ons
- Stackable
- No per-GB billing
- No overage fees

Add-ons are not available on the free tier.

---

## Backups

Automatic backups are included on paid plans.

Backups:
- Run on a schedule
- Are retained for a defined window
- Can be downloaded by the user
- Are included in storage usage

Backups are designed for recovery and portability, not archival storage.

---

## Principle

> Plans define how you work.  
> Storage defines how much you keep.
