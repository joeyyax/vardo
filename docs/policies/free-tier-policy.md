# Free Tier Policy

The free tier exists to help users evaluate whether Scope fits how they work.

It is intentionally limited.  
It is not intended to support long-term business operations.

---

## Purpose of Free

Free is for:
- Learning Scope’s model
- Setting up projects
- Tracking light usage
- Evaluating fit with real work

Free is not for:
- Ongoing client operations
- Collaboration
- Automation or reliability guarantees
- Long-term dependency

---

## Free Tier Limits

- 1 user
- Up to **3 external clients**
- **Unlimited internal work**
- Limited storage
- No collaboration
- No automatic backups
- Manual export only

These limits are explicit and enforced.

---

## Internal Work on Free

Internal work is always allowed on the free tier.

This includes:
- Personal projects
- Internal tools
- Planning and setup
- Research and experimentation

Internal work:
- Does **not** count toward external client limits
- Is tracked and reported normally
- Is never invoiced

Free users may rely on Scope for internal work indefinitely.

---

## Storage on Free

Free includes a small storage allowance intended for:
- Receipts
- Light attachments
- Evaluation use

Free users cannot purchase storage add-ons.

When storage limits are reached, users must upgrade to continue.

---

## Upgrade Philosophy

Free users upgrade when:
- They exceed client limits
- They need collaboration
- They want automatic backups
- They rely on Scope for real client work

Free should feel useful, but incomplete.

---

## Principle

> Free reduces product risk, not business risk.
