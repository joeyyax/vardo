# Migration Assistance Options

Migration assistance is optional. You are always free to export your application data and migrate independently.

---

## Self-Service (Included)
- Automated application data export
- No direct support

Best if you or your new provider will handle everything.

---

## Guided Migration
Billed at standard hourly rates.

May include:
- Answering questions about the exported data
- Explaining deployment or configuration details
- Coordinating timing of final exports

Does not include hands-on deployment or debugging.

---

## Hands-On Migration (Limited)
Requires written agreement and scoped approval.

May include:
- Assisting with deployment to a new environment
- Coordinating cutover timing
- Limited validation after migration

Does not include rebuilding, re-architecting, or ongoing support unless separately agreed.

---

## Notes
- Compatibility with new environments is not guaranteed
- Migration complexity varies by project
- We’re happy to talk through options before anything changes
