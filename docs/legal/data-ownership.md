# Data Ownership & Access

Client retains full ownership of all data associated with their application.

---

## What This Means

- Your data is always yours
- You may request a copy at any time
- You are not locked into Provider’s hosting

---

## What You Can Request

Upon request, Provider can supply:
- Application source code as deployed
- A database backup
- Uploaded media and assets

Data is provided in standard formats intended to support migration or archiving.

---

## Timing

Provider will make reasonable efforts to fulfill data requests promptly. Large sites or complex systems may require additional time.

---

## Questions

If you have questions about access, backups, or data portability, just ask.
