# Approvals Model

This document defines where formal approvals exist in the system and what they mean.

---

## What Requires Approval

Approvals are used for:
- Proposal acceptance
- Contract acceptance

Optional (configurable):
- Deliverable or milestone approval

---

## What Approval Means

An approval is:
- Explicit
- Intentional
- Recorded

Approval confirms shared understanding and agreement.

---

## What Approval Is Not

Approval is not:
- A comment saying “looks good”
- Moving a task to Done
- Silence or inactivity
- Implicit consent

If it matters legally or financially, it requires a formal approval action.

---

## Approval Mechanics

- Approvals happen via dedicated actions
- Approval state is visible
- Timestamps are recorded
- Notifications are sent on approval or rejection

---

## Summary

Approvals mark decision points.

They exist to reduce ambiguity, not add friction.
