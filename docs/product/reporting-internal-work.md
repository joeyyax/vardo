# Reporting Internal Work

Internal work appears in reports by default.

This is intentional.

---

## Included by Default

Internal work contributes to:
- Daily, weekly, and monthly totals
- Time distribution reports
- Trend analysis
- Utilization metrics
- Cost-of-time calculations

Internal work is part of reality.

---

## Client-Facing Exclusions

Internal work is excluded from:
- Client invoices
- Client-facing reports
- Client portals
- External exports (unless explicitly included)

Internal visibility ≠ external visibility.

---

## Filtering and Views

Users may:
- Filter internal work in reports
- Compare internal vs external time
- Understand effort vs revenue gaps

Hiding internal work should be a conscious choice, not the default.

---

## Summary

Internal work should be visible internally and invisible externally.

Scope draws that line clearly.
