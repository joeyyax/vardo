# Opinionated Decisions

These decisions are intentional and protect the integrity of the system.

---

## We Believe

- Structure reduces friction
- Visibility builds trust
- Fewer options create better outcomes

---

## We Do Not Support

- Client-controlled workflows
- Skipping lifecycle steps
- Per-client workflow customization
- Nested or hybrid projects
- Implicit work starts

---

## Guiding Rule

If a feature adds flexibility at the cost of clarity, it does not belong.

---

## Summary

This product favors calm, predictable workflows over infinite configurability.
